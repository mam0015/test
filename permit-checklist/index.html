Before starting work, provide the complete scope, plans and title information to the relevant local council and an appointed registered building surveyor. Written confirmation from the appropriate professional or authority should always take priority over this checklist.

Minimum verification path

  1. Download the property’s Planning Property Report.
  2. Ask the local council planning/building team about planning controls and local-law permits.
  3. Appoint a registered building surveyor to decide the building-permit path.
  4. Confirm trade certificates with licensed practitioners.
  5. Do not start until required approvals are issued.
